[Leagify](https://leagify.github.io) is a platform for building fantasy leagues out of anything. This site tracks one of its longest-running games: a fantasy NFL Draft league that's been going since 2014.

Each spring, six owners — Ross, Tilo, Jawad, Jared, AJ, and Netten — hold an auction draft to claim college football programs. When the real NFL Draft happens, owners earn points for every player selected from their schools, with bigger points for earlier picks and a bonus for traded picks. The owner whose schools produce the most NFL talent wins.

This site updates automatically throughout draft weekend so you can follow the scores in real time.
